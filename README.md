# Chustomer Churn Analysis-Excel-Power Query
![My Image](https://github.com/rtrahulthapa9-byte/Customer-Churn-Analysis-Excel-Power-Query/blob/ce129d12297b262be1166048e59ae7fb5ecd4e36/Screenshot%202026-07-16%20121240.png)
An end-to-end analysis of 2,800 subscription customers — identifying why customers leave and what drives retention across Basic, Standard, and Premium plans.

**Total Customer**: 2800      **Time Period**:2023-2024     **Data Source**:Kaggle     **Tools Used**:Excel-Power Query

**Project Objective**

The objective of this project was to analyze customer churn patterns, monitor customer usage behavior, compare subscription plan performance, identify factors affecting customer retention, and create an interactive dashboard that provides clear business insights

**Key Insights**

🔹 Premium plan has the highest churn rate, closely followed by Standard, while Basic has the lowest churn among all three plans.

🔹 Overall churn rate stands at 57.32%, meaning more than half of total customers have churned.

🔹 Basic plan users spend the most time on the platform (03:45:36 avg weekly usage)

🔹 Premium plan contributes the largest revenue share at 54%, followed by Standard at 31% and Basic at 15%

🔹 Churn by quarter stayed fairly stable between 25%-26%, with Qtr 1 being the lowest at 23%.
